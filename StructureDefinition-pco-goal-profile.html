Resource Profile: Person-Centered Goal

Official URL: http://hl7.org/fhir/us/pco/StructureDefinition/pco-goal-profile Version: 0.2.0
Draft as of 2025-02-03 Computable Name: PCOGoalProfile

Person-centered goal focused on an individual's well-being. A Person-centered goal SHALL include either a PCO category, or address a What Matters assessment.

Usage:

  • Derived from this Resource Profile: Person-Centered GAS Goal and Person-Centered PROM Goal
  • Refer to this Resource Profile: Person-Centered Care Plan and Personal Priorities Organizer
  • Examples for this Resource Profile: Goal/pcoGoalPainLevelExample
